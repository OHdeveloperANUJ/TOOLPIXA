# Project-Scoped Rules for ToolPixa Workspace

All development, copywriting, and feature design tasks in this workspace must strictly adhere to the following rules:

---

## 1. UI & STYLING COMPLIANCE
- **Rule**: Every user interface component must look premium and high-end. Never use default unstyled browser elements or generic, flat single-color blocks.
- **Reference**: Follow the instructions in the [Premium Aesthetics & Styling Skill](file:///C:/Users/Srijan/.gemini/antigravity/scratch/toolpixa-master/.agents/skills/premium-aesthetics-css/SKILL.md).

## 2. REACT STATE & MATH CALCULATION SAFETY
- **Rule**: Calculators must calculate their output immediately on mount. Zero-value bugs, unformatted decimal floats, and UI math crashes (like `NaN` or `Infinity`) are strictly unacceptable. Keep inputs and sliders synced.
- **Reference**: Follow the instructions in the [React State & Math Safety Skill](file:///C:/Users/Srijan/.gemini/antigravity/scratch/toolpixa-master/.agents/skills/react-state-math-safety/SKILL.md).

## 3. DATA VISUALIZATION AND CHARTS
- **Rule**: All charts must be fully responsive, work beautifully in both light and dark modes, use linear gradients where appropriate, and utilize custom styled tooltips.
- **Reference**: Follow the instructions in the [Recharts Visualization Standards Skill](file:///C:/Users/Srijan/.gemini/antigravity/scratch/toolpixa-master/.agents/skills/recharts-visualization-standards/SKILL.md).

## 4. CONTENT AUTHENTICITY AND COPYWRITING
- **Rule**: Eliminate generic, repetitive AI fluff. Provide detailed step-by-step mathematical walkthroughs showing formulas in LaTeX, manual calculation steps, and realistic persona-based scenarios. Provide high-quality, specific FAQs.
- **Reference**: Follow the instructions in the [Anti-AI Slop Content Quality Skill](file:///C:/Users/Srijan/.gemini/antigravity/scratch/toolpixa-master/.agents/skills/anti-ai-slop-content/SKILL.md).

## 5. DOCUMENT EXPORT STANDARDS
- **Rule**: All CSV and PDF exports must follow professional-grade layouts, repeat table headers, prevent text-wrapping and horizontal truncation, and utilize BOM prefixes for Excel CSV compatibility.
- **Reference**: Follow the instructions in the [PDF & CSV Export Standards Skill](file:///C:/Users/Srijan/.gemini/antigravity/scratch/toolpixa-master/.agents/skills/pdf-csv-export-standards/SKILL.md).
